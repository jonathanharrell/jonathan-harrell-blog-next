In the middle of the long-grass woods,<br/>
There is a gilded chapel,<br/>
Cross pointing to open sky.<br/>
I think I prefer the nave of leaning trees,<br/>
The transept of fallen branches,<br/>
A holy altar of wind-carved stone.
