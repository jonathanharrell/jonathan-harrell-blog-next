> I think I have been<br/>
> sad every afternoon of my life.<br/>
> Outside a child runs in the grass.<br/>
> Soon I will appear and call her to me.
> <cite><i>[Golden Hour](https://poems.com/poem/golden-hour/)</i> by Jennifer Polson Peterson, excerpt</cite>
