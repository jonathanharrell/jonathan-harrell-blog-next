> and ask again, <em>who’s going to teach me now</em>? or hold up<br/>

> <cite><i>[Picture of a Boy, Looking Away (Gaza, 2015)](https://poems.com/poem/picture-of-a-boy-looking-away-gaza-2015/)</i> by Marjorie Lotfi</cite>
