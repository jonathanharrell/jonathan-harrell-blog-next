> By which he meant light,<br/>
> the lightness of air: the lob and lift and score.<br/><br/>
> The swelling crowd.<br/>
> The flicker first before the bell<br/>
> blaring into the night.
> <cite><i>[The Boys of Cass and Pacific](https://poems.com/poem/the-boys-of-cass-and-pacific)</i> by Luke Johnson, excerpt</cite>
