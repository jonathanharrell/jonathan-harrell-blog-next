> But I knew the end, had already been there.<br/>
> Touched its elongated snout, the awful hairs.<br/>
> Watched it pushing its pie tin around<br/>
> the backyard, its tiny crossed eyes so close<br/>
> to the ground.
> <cite><i>[Ars Poetica](https://poems.com/poem/ars-poetica-4)</i> by Bridget Lowe, excerpt</cite>
