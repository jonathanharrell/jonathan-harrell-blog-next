> an altar pulses open: some ancient bloodlove unfurls<br/>
> what breath remembers: my body of her body and all<br/>
> we come from and come from and come from, how<br/>
> each of us curves with the world, blown like a leaf
> <cite><i>[When I hear my mama sing](https://poems.com/poem/when-i-hear-my-mama-sing)</i> by Aaron Coleman, excerpt</cite>
