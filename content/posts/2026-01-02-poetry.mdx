> I knocked on the afterlife, once<br/>
> to open the door, twice to bid him bye,<br/><br/>
> thrice to say *look at me*,<br/>
> gift me the house of remembrance.
> <cite><i>[Wishbone](https://poems.com/poem/wishbone)</i> by Romeo Oriogun, excerpt</cite>
