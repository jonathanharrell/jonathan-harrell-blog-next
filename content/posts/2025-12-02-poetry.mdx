> Time was so slow and so thick<br/>
> my word-sprung yearning<br/>
> rode its drifts until, overpowered<br/>
> by hunger, I fell asleep in the sun.<br/>
> I did not know that I was happy.
> <cite><i>[After Turning the Clocks Back](https://poems.com/poem/After-Turning-the-Clocks-Back)</i> by Jennifer Moxley, excerpt</cite>
