> I heard my voice from below:<br/>
> It called me by my name.<br/><br/>
> I ran downstairs.<br/>
> When I arrived, I was dead.<br/><br/>
> Carrying myself on my back<br/>
> I climbed up with myself.
> <cite><i>[The Stairs](https://poems.com/poem/the-stairs)</i> by Ulalume González de León, translated from Spanish by Terry Ehret & Nancy J. Morales, excerpt</cite>
