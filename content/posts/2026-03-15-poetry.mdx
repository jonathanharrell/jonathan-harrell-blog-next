> I steal faces<br/>
> and keep them in the branches. […]<br/>
> I embrace a germ of water,<br/>
> create new ways<br/>
> to be born into other families<br/>
> like a small offering.
> <cite><i>[Childhood](https://poems.com/poem/childhood)</i> by Luciana Jazmín Coronado, translated from Spanish by Allison A. deFreese, excerpt</cite>
