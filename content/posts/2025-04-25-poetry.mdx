> Who can blame them for believing<br/>
> what they saw? Bodies<br/>
> holding each other in the night,<br/>
> against the darkness.<br/>
> I have no excuse.<br/>
> I was there, and I was also dancing.
> <cite><i>[The Inventors](https://poems.com/poem/the-inventors)</i> by Arielle Hebert, excerpt</cite>
