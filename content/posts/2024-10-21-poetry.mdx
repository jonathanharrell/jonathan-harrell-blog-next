> in the dream, I wake<br/>
> treading the underwater<br/>
> in the black beneath I see<br/><br/>
> like horses galloping<br/>
> the gloom of the sea
> <cite><i>[Qasida for Abdel Wahab Yousif](https://poems.com/poem/qasida-for-abdel-wahab-yousif/)</i> by Philip Metres, excerpt</cite>
