> There is a country I see on the internet<br/>
> and in the street outside I see a country<br/>
> there is also a country I see in my mind<br/><br/>
> I dream about this country more than anyone I ever loved<br/>
> it looks just like a glowing constellation&emsp;exactly like God
> <cite><i>[Historical Material 2020](https://poems.com/poem/historical-material-2020/)</i> by Lena Tsykynovska, excerpt</cite>
