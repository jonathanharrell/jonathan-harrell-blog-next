> A dolphin falls in love,<br/>
> And there are a million<br/>
> Pregnancies in the minds of men.<br/>
> A salmon’s leap<br/>
> Is a philosopher’s insight.<br/>
> Cosmologies are born<br/>
> When statues dance.<br/>
> A poet’s thought<br/>
> Unfurls unknown constellations.
> <cite><i>[Wild](https://poems.com/poem/wild/)</i> by Ben Okri, excerpt</cite>
