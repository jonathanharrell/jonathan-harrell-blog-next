The water come close.<br/>
Every year it come closer.<br/>
I grow up, I get old, I feel the pull strong,<br/>
Back out sea-side.<br/>
One day the tide come<br/>
And I got nothing to hold on to.<br/>
I keep thinking of an old shore,<br/>
Long gone washed away.
