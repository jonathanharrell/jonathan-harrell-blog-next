That place, that childhood beach,<br/>
Where you first learned to love and fear the sea...<br/>
Go there.<br/>
Stand at the shore.<br/>
Let your feet sink into the sand.<br/>
And listen,<br/>
Listen again,<br/>
To the waves<br/>
Endlessly calling you home.
