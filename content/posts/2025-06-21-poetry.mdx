> elderberry trees&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;moths crawl over&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;their hay-scratched hands<br/><br/>
> their eyes&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;pulling new shapes&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;from tree-shadows<br/>
> the shapes whatever&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;they want them to be
