> How do we bid farewell to him,<br/>
> no longer among us? [ . . . ]<br/>
> How do we bid farewell to the one<br/>
> we can never speak to again, we speak only of him? [ . . . ]<br/>
> Who is still here among us, as dust and ashes.
> <cite><i>[3.1 Mourning Verse, on the Death of Lajos Szuromi](https://poems.com/poem/3-1-mourning-verse-on-the-death-of-lajos-szuromi-excerpt/)</i> by Szilárd Borbély, excerpt</cite>
