> Banging the blue shutters–night-rain;<br/>
> and a deep gash opened in the yard. […]<br/>
> I looked up.<br/>
> That’s where one looks in the grip of a dream.
> <cite><i>[Largesse](https://poems.com/poem/largesse)</i> by Ellen Bryant Voigt, excerpt</cite>
