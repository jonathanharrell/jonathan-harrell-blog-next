> break the windows come<br/>
> with your roots and your worms<br/>
> spread yourself over our wishes<br/>
> our waste-sorting systems our protheses<br/>
> and outstanding payments<br/>
> cover us with your rustling greenery<br/>
> and your spores cover us that we may<br/>
> become green
> <cite>Come wilderness into our homes by Daniela Danz, excerpt</cite>
