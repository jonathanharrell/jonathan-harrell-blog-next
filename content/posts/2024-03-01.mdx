Sometimes,<br/>
The dream is the thing:<br/>
Shadow-split light<br/>
Over a staircase<br/>
That leads nowhere.<br/>
Me, standing in a dark room,<br/>
With no place to go,<br/>
Speaking to someone else<br/>
Who isn't there.
