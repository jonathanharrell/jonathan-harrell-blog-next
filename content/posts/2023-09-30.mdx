When I stood beneath the moon,<br/>
I wept,<br/>
Knowing how perfect the moment was:<br/>
The silver air,<br/>
Waves swelling in the dark tides,<br/>
About to break,<br/>
Before they froze,<br/>
Like glass,<br/>
In my memory.
