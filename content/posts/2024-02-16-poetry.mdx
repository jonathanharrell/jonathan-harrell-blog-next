> *Your brother is lost*, my mother says, *because we didn’t believe him*.<br/>
> *He told us there was loud humming inside the walls*—Go to sleep, *we said*.<br/>
> *And he couldn’t*<br/>
> *couldn’t go to sleep*.<br/>
> <cite><i>[Misinformation](https://poems.com/poem/misinformation/)</i> by Diana Khoi Nguyen, excerpt</cite>
