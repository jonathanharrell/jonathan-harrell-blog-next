Noxious fumes rise from a green pool,<br/>
At the heart of Mount Aso.<br/>
Pinpoint bugs bite my arms, my neck.

![Thick clouds of steam rise from a rocky volcanic crater, with swirling vapors obscuring the crater floor filled with pale yellow water.](/assets/photos/20130607-IMG_8128.jpg)

![Clouds of steam emerge from the steep ridges of a volcanic landscape, against a cloudy sky.](/assets/photos/20130607-IMG_8123.jpg)

I see land that was vomited, spewed, and spattered.

![The bottom of a volcanic crater with blackened earth and streaks of white mineral deposits cutting through the rocky basin.](/assets/photos/20130607-IMG_8170.jpg)

![A view of rugged, steep cliffs and ash-laden slopes of a volcanic crater, showcasing layers of rock formations and patches of greenery.](/assets/photos/20130607-IMG_8185.jpg)

Still people make a home there,<br/>
Growing rice in the mountain shadow.

![An old, weathered wooden barn sits beside a flooded rice paddy with the silhouette of distant mountains rising under an overcast sky.](/assets/photos/20130607-IMG_8069.jpg)
